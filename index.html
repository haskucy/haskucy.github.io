Introduction

I am a programmer with a focus on general programming, web development, and machine learning, paired with a dedicated interest in mathematical research and puzzle-solving. This platform highlights my journey, maybe as projects, or maybe just me sharing my thought.

Interest

  • Exploring Ideas: I enjoy reading books and learning across disciplines, particularly in epistemology, metacognition, and related theoretical concepts.
  • Leisure: I mostly read manga/manhwa like One Piece, Frieren, Dandadan, The Greatest Estate Designer, and many more. Occasionally I'd like to play Wesnoth in my spare time.
