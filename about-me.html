About Myself

Glad that you are willing to know me...

A Short Description

My name is Rendya Yuschak, also known online as Haskucy. I hold a Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Indonesia and currently co-found Dr. Komputer, an IT support and hardware repair business in Cikarang, Indonesia. My work spans web development, machine learning, and IT consulting, with a focus on pragmatic, reliable solutions.

Skills and Expertise

  • General Programming & Software Engineering: Proficient in multiple languages including Python, Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, C, C#, and Haskell, with a solid foundation in algorithm design, functional programming, and numerical analysis.
  • Web Development & Database Management: Experienced in building web applications using frameworks such as Django, Laravel, Node.js, React.js, and Spring Boot. Skilled in managing databases like PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, and Hadoop, ensuring robust and scalable solutions.
  • Data Science, Machine Learning & AI: Competent in employing TensorFlow, PyTorch, Matplotlib, and Pandas to tackle projects in machine learning and artificial intelligence, along with a keen interest in data-driven research and analysis.
  • IT Support & Business Operations: Experienced in providing IT support and hardware repair, as demonstrated by co-founding an IT support business, with hands-on experience in system maintenance, troubleshooting, and business management.
  • UI/UX & Multimedia Design: Adept at designing intuitive user interfaces and digital content using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, and Illustrator, complemented by experience in video editing and multimedia production.
