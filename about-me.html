About Myself

Glad that you are willing to know me...

A Short Description

My name is Rendya Yuschak, also known online as Haskucy. I am a developer with a passion for programming, mathematics, and problem-solving. While this website is still a work in progress, it reflects my enthusiasm for technology and continuous learning.

Background

I specialize in web development, machine learning, and data science. My technical expertise spans front-end and back-end development, database management, and scientific computing. I am also deeply interested in mathematics, particularly combinatorics, logic, and discrete math, which I integrate into my technical projects whenever possible.

Interests and Aspirations

Beyond coding, I am fascinated by natural language processing, data visualization, and math proof verification programs like Lean 4. I also explore the theory of understanding knowledge, including epistemology and metacognition. My ultimate goal is to contribute meaningfully to technology and education, bridging the gap between abstract concepts and practical applications.

What Drives Me

Curiosity and a commitment to excellence drive my work. Whether solving complex problems or exploring new ideas, I strive to create impactful solutions that blend innovation with practicality.
